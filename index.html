Affordable Housing Projects

The Apartments at Oak Meadow Park is a proposed four-story affordable housing development located southeast of Oak Meadow Park, near public transportation, grocery stores, schools, and other key amenities. The development consists of 62 total units, with a mix of affordable and market-rate units, split based on current monthly income (CMI). The affordable units will be funded through federal tax credits and tax incremental financing. The development is expected to be a popular choice for young families seeking affordable housing in the Madison area and offers a detailed analysis of its future prospects and financing methods.
